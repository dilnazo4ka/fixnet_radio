INTERNET BY RADIO


FOR BUSINESS

Using a radio channel is an ideal solution for connecting to the Internet at remote sites or in the absence of the possibility of connecting via fiber in the shortest possible time. The connection technology consists of installation on the roof of a building or an existing antenna-mast structure that connects to the NetFix base station. The Internet over the radio channel in Kazakhstan is generally similar in principle to a Wi-Fi bridge: the signal is also transmitted from a powerful source connected to the Internet using a cable to the receiver.
